About Us

We are two friends who love to travel. We both met in college and wanted to start our own travel blog. After graduation, we both decided to take time off to travel the world and we don't regret one minute of it. Over the years, we've learned so much about each other that it truly strengthened our relationship. This blog started with just the both of us and now we expanded to have a team of writers. You'll find tips on how to travel without breaking the bank (for our budget babes!) and everything in between. Travel safe, Queens!
